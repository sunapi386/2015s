Argument Outline
Preamble
Value: 7% of the final grade
Due date: end of lecture on June 10
The outline is to be done on your own time. There will be no class time dedicated to the outline. 
Bring a printed copy of your outline to lecture on June 3. 
Marks will not be released until the final project is handed in.
An example outline will be presented in lecture on June 1. 
Learning Outcomes
By the end of this assessment, the student will:

State a tentative thesis;
State a minimum of four (4) reasons for the thesis;
Defend each of the stated reasons.
Assessment Description
This exercise will prepare you for your first draft of your essay. It should be approximately 1 to 2 pages in length.
